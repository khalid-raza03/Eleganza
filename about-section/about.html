About 'Eleganza'


Eleganza is an Indian e-commerce company and a subsidiary of fashion Industries. Founded in 2022, it is the largest e-commerce in India in terms of revenue. a fashion and lifestyle brand, is Reliance Retail's digital commerce initiative and is the ultimate fashion destination for styles that are handpicked, on trend and at prices that are the best you'll find anywhere.. As of 2023, it has over 2450 employees at 1800 store locations in 70 towns.
